# 💸 Payment & Bounty Policy

This project is fiscally sponsored by **[HCB](https://hcb.hackclub.com)**. Our finances are **100% transparent** and can be viewed by the public at our [Transparency Dashboard](https://hcb.hackclub.comprojects/YOUR-PROJECT-SLUG).

## 🛠 How We Pay
We use a **Bounty + Maintenance** model to ensure the project stays healthy and contributors are rewarded fairly for their impact.

### 1. Issue Bounties (Task-Based)
Look for labels on GitHub issues to see the reward amount:
*   `bounty: $5` – **Junior/Starter**: Documentation fixes, typos, or simple CSS/UI tweaks.
*   `bounty: $20` – **Standard**: Bug fixes, small new features, or unit tests.
*   **Tiered Bounties**: Complex tasks or security patches are priced individually based on seniority and effort.

**The Process:**
1.  **Claim**: Comment on the issue to claim it. A maintainer must acknowledge you before you start.
2.  **Code**: Submit a Pull Request (PR) referencing the issue.
3.  **Merge**: Once the PR is reviewed and merged, you are eligible for payment.
4.  **Invoice**: Request payment via our HCB dashboard and **must** include the link to your merged PR.

### 2. Maintenance & Oversight (Time-Based)
The **Project Lead** and **Senior Maintainers** are paid an hourly rate for work that cannot be "bountied," such as:
*   Strategic planning and roadmap management.
*   Reviewing PRs and mentoring junior developers.
*   Server maintenance and security monitoring.

*Our current Lead rate is **$40/hr**, capped at **20 hours/week** to ensure fund sustainability.*

---

## 🧾 How to Get Paid
Once your work is merged, follow these steps:

1.  **Join the Org**: Ask the Lead for an invite to the HCB organization.
2.  **Submit Request**: Go to our [HCB Payout Page](https://hcb.hackclub.comprojects/YOUR-PROJECT-SLUG/payouts).
3.  **Attach Proof**: Upload a simple PDF invoice and **link the GitHub PR/Issue** in the description.
4.  **Approval**: The Project Lead will review and approve within 48–72 hours.

## ⚖️ Rules & Transparency
*   **No Hidden Fees**: We do not pay for work that isn't documented on GitHub.
*   **Public Ledger**: Every payment made to a developer is visible to our donors.
*   **Final Say**: The Project Lead has the final say on whether a bounty's requirements were met.
