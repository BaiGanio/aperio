Llama 3.1 8B tools · the threshold

The most important step in the local-AI journey. At 8B, tool calling becomes reliable — this is the first model that genuinely remembers you across chats, reads your files, and browses the web. It stops being a chat toy and becomes an assistant that does things.

Hardware

Where hardware starts to matter. Because it can use tools, conversations grow longer (file contents and tool results all consume context), so a roomy context window helps.

  • Apple Silicon · 16 GB+The ideal experience — the model fits fully in GPU-accessible memory with headroom. On 32 GB it never spills to swap.
  • PC · NVIDIA GPU, 8 GB+ VRAMAn RTX 2070 / 3060 or better holds it comfortably. A 6 GB card works but may spill to system RAM.
  • PC · CPU onlyUsable but slower — ~5–10 words/sec on a recent i7 / Ryzen 7. Noticeably less snappy than on a GPU.

Raise the context window in .env — especially important here, since tools fill it fast:

OLLAMA_NUM_CTX=32768

Try it yourself

Everything that failed on the chat-only models should start working here. Tool calling is ~85% reliable — when a call misses, naming the tool gets it back on track.

1 · Memory across chats — the big unlock

The single biggest capability jump in the whole lineup. This should just work.

Paste thisPlease remember this: my favourite season is autumn, and I grew up in a town called Hirakata.
Then, new chatOpen a fresh conversation and paste: "What do you know about me from memory?"
✅ GoodIt calls recall and accurately reports autumn + Hirakata in the new chat. The memory persists because it lives in Aperio's database, not the conversation.
❌ The wallNothing recalled on first try. Retry, or be explicit: "Use the recall tool to find any memories about me."
→ MeansOnce memory works, everything built on tool calling works: files, wiki, web. You're no longer limited to one conversation.

2 · Reach past its 2023 cutoff

Llama 3.1's training stops around December 2023 — older than the Qwen models. But it can fetch the live web, which more than makes up for it.

Paste thisSearch the web and tell me who won the most recent FIFA World Cup, with the year and a source link.
✅ GoodIt uses fetch_url and returns the current answer with a source — instead of stopping at its 2023 knowledge.
❌ The wallIt answers from memory and gives an out-of-date result. Retry naming the web tool.
→ MeansAn older cutoff with web access beats a newer cutoff without one. Lean on fetch for anything recent.

3 · A real multi-tool workflow

The day-to-day reason you stepped up to 16 GB: the model storing context, writing documentation, and reading your project — in one go.

Paste thisI have a project called "Garden Plan 2026." Please remember that. Then write a short wiki article called "Garden Plan 2026" with a project overview, three goals for this year, and a list of supplies I might need. Finally, tell me what files and folders you can see in the project directory.
✅ GoodIt uses remember, then wiki_write (it may ask you to approve the write), then scan_project / read_file. It should attempt all three and succeed at at least two.
❌ The wallIt drops a step or runs them out of order. At 8B, very long chains can lose the thread.
→ MeansKeep chains to 2–3 tools. For longer, more reliable workflows, the 12B and 30B tiers are the next step.

4 · Reason through a few steps

A generalist — competent at multi-step reasoning, if not as deep as the larger tiers.

Paste thisA shelf has 12 books. I remove 4, then add twice as many as I removed, then give away half of all the books now on the shelf. How many are left? Show your steps.
✅ Good12 − 4 = 8, + 8 = 16, half of 16 = 8 left — with the steps shown.
❌ The wallA bare number, or steps that don't add up. Watch for confident wrongness.
→ MeansFine for everyday multi-step logic; double-check anything that really matters.

The verdict

Llama 3.1 8B is the threshold model: the smallest one that turns Aperio from a chat window into an assistant. It's a reliable generalist that trades a little creative flair for solid tool use. If 16 GB is your ceiling, this is the best experience within it. Creative writing your priority instead? A Qwen 4B may actually please you more.
