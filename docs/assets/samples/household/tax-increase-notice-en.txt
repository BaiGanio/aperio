ENERGY AND WATER REGULATORY COMMISSION (sample)
Official notice to consumers

Date: 30.06.2026
Ref. No: REG-2026-0742

SUBJECT: Change to the regulated prices of utility services

Dear consumers,

We hereby inform you that, effective 01.07.2026, the regulated
prices of the following utility services will increase by
7.4 % (seven point four percent):

  - electricity for household use
  - water supply and sewerage
  - central heating

The increase applies only to the utility services listed above.
Prices for fuel, groceries, internet and transport are not
affected by this decision.

Worked example: for monthly utility spending of BGN 260.50, the
7.4 % increase results in an additional BGN 19.28 per month
(new amount BGN 279.78).

This notice is for information only. For the exact amounts,
please refer to your next monthly invoice.
